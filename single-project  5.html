Meet EatMeFirst: your free anti-waste kitchen companion

Tool

EatMeFirst

Price

Free · no account needed

Topic

Take action

The single biggest cause of food waste at home is simple: we forget what we already have. Food gets pushed to the back of the fridge, expiry dates sneak up on us, and good food ends up in the bin. EatMeFirst is a free tool we built to fix exactly that.

Add what's in your kitchen and EatMeFirst sorts everything by what to eat first, colour-codes items that are about to expire, reminds you before food goes bad, suggests "use-it-up" meal ideas, and even counts how much money you've saved from the bin. There's no account required and nothing to install. It runs right in your browser and keeps your data on your own device. Less waste, more meals, and a little less guilt every week.

Meet EatMeFirst: your free anti-waste fridge tracker

Because forgetting what you have is the #1 cause of home food waste

Meet EatMeFirst: your free anti-waste fridge tracker

By Linda 2 June 2024

Most household food waste comes down to one simple thing: we forget what we already have. EatMeFirst is a free tool built to fix exactly that.

You cannot eat what you forget you own.

Add what's in your kitchen and EatMeFirst sorts everything by what to eat first, warns you before food expires, suggests "use-it-up" meals, and counts the money you save from the bin. Less waste, more meals, no guilt.

It takes a minute to start and works with whatever you already have at home, no special shopping required.
